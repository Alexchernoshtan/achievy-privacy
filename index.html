We collect the following types of information:

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as features used, chores completed, and rewards earned, to improve the App experience and provide analytics.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase cloud infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

5. Children's Privacy

Achievy is designed as a family app. Children's accounts are created and managed by parents/guardians within the family group. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents can manage, review, and delete their children's data at any time through the App.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at chernoshtan84@gmail.com. Upon deletion request, we will remove your data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the App.
