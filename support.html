Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a family?

After signing up, tap "Create Family" on the home screen. You can then invite family members by sharing a join code.

How do subscriptions work?

Achievy offers Starter, Plus, and Family subscription plans with monthly and yearly options. All plans include a 14-day free trial. You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, go to Settings on your iPhone > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Achievy > Cancel Subscription.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, go to Settings within the Achievy app > Account > Delete Account. Alternatively, email us at chernoshtan84@gmail.com.

Is the app safe for children?

Yes. Children's accounts are managed by parents within the family group. We follow strict privacy guidelines and do not collect unnecessary data from children.
